about

Hi! Thanks for stopping by.

My name’s Lucy Kuo.
I was ​born and raised in Taipei, Taiwan.

I graduated from National Taiwan University (NTU) in 2015 and obtained double bachelor’s degree in Biology and Economics.

In July 2015, I decided to follow my dreams and interviewed for a Designer position at Asus Computer. And I got it.
For almost 2 years, I learned not only how to be part of a top-notch design team but also how to boost my creativity.

I’ve since moved on from my work to pursue a new gig.

On this site, you can enjoy reading some of my pieces, which will be added regularly.

And you can also take a look at my Resume.

If you want to know more about me, feel free to Contact me.
